~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ Behind the Runtime v1.0.0.0 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Original version of Behind the Runtime, before I started adding way too much A/C/E bloat in the updated version of the extension. Also the first extension I ever released.

--- SUPPORTED RUNTIMES ---

- Windows (Unicode + ANSI)
- Html5 (Runtime V1 only)
- UWP (All variations)
- Flash
- XNA (All variations)

--- UNFINISHED RUNTIME PORTS ---

- Android
- iOS
- MacOS

--- PLANNED RUNTIME PORTS ---

- Haxe | Linux + WebGL
- Java | All variations

-- CANCELLED RUNTIME PORTS --- 

- N/A
